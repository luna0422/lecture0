Then someone had a bad idea..

But at the end of the 40s the sovjets decided to build irrigation canals, along the streams which the Aral sea was fed by. They did this, so they can produce rice, melons and cotton on the drought areas of the Kara-kum desert.
The idea was made happen and it brought results with it. Well, Uzbekistan did not become the world's greatest rice producer, but because of the immoderate water abstraction, the sea's level began to sink unstoppably.
